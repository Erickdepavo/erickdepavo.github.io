About

Hi! I’m Erick Daniel Padilla. I’m an 18-year-old CS student from Culiacán, Mexico who loves programming and technology in general. I’ve been a Swift developer since 2017, and since then I’ve been doing some work that I would like to showcase to the world. That’s the reason I made this website.

I am currently both a designer and a developer. This site, as well as all of my apps, was fully designed and coded by me, which can give you a good idea of what I can do.

Over these few years, I’ve been working on many projects, not only from mine but from other people as well, such as Enrútate.
