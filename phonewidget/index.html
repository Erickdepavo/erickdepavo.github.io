Phone Widget

Your contacts, directly on the home screen.

Phone Widget lets you have quick access to the ones who matter to you. Two taps are what you need to begin a call: One on the widget, and one on the contact screen.

You’ll never miss the old one.

When iOS 14 was launched, Apple removed the widget that came with the phone app. However, with our many widgets options and their beautiful design, you will never look back.

Intuitive is my second name.

Our user interface makes it super easy to add widgets and manage them: it doesn’t matter if you want to add, delete, or move a contact, it's just blazing fast to do.

Unlimited, just like your imagination.

Phone Widget Unlimited is what you want to enjoy our app at its full potential. With a one-time payment of $0.99 USD, you’ll unlock unlimited widget slots and up to 10 contacts per widget (enough to fit a large-sized widget!).

Privacy matters.

We take privacy seriously. Our app doesn’t contain any trackers and we don’t really collect any information from you.
