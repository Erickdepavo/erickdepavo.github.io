Phone Widget

Privacy Policy

Regarding data collection

Phone Widget by itself does not collect any personal data from the user. Phone Widget does not upload any content to any service, and local storage is used exclusively for the app’s settings.

Regarding the use of the device’s contacts

Phone Widget uses its access to the device’s contacts for the sole purpose of widget displaying using the iOS Widgets feature.
