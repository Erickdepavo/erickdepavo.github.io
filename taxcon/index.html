Taxcon

Using Taxcon, shopping in the United States is just a lot easier, as it calculates sales taxes for you and converts prices to any currency if you wish.

Taxcon calculates sales taxes for you, letting you know how much you will pay before arriving at the cashier. In addition, it lets you apply the percentage and money discounts you can find in your way.

However, we think this is not enough. That’s why Taxcon Pro is now available as an in-app purchase.

Taxcon Pro includes:

  • Ads removal
  • Calculator built-in into the app
  • Precaptured Taxes for all the US
  • Automatic Tax selection based on GPS (Only in the US)
  • Unlimited number of discounts
